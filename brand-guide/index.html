DO / DON'T
DO

Use Bebas Neue for all headings, CTAs, and display text.

DON'T

Use script fonts, colored text outside the three-color palette, or rounded rectangle shapes.

DO

Use pill shapes (border-radius: 100px) for all interactive elements.

DON'T

Use sharp corners or square buttons. Everything should feel soft and approachable.

DO

Keep all text uppercase. The voice is confident and direct.

DON'T

Mix case. No sentence case, no title case. All caps, always.

DO

Use the logo on both light and dark backgrounds — it works on both.

DON'T

Recolor, rotate, stretch, or add effects to the logo. Use it as-is.
